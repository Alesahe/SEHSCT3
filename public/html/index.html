Welcome to Vivid Hills Family Day Care

Vivid Hills is based in our warm and welcoming home in Baulkham Hills. Our space is designed for safety and exploration, with a dedicated play area filled with age-appropriate toys and resources. We also have a newly renovated backyard where children enjoy outdoor play, gardening and nature exploration.

As a family, we value safety, kindness, respect and open communication. I involve my husband and two children in the day care, creating a supportive environment where your child(ren) will feel like part of our family.

We love sharing our interests, which include role-playing, arts and crafts, sensory activities, music, dancing, reading, STEM and outdoor activities, which are incorporated into daily activities.
